CURRENT WEATHER-HEALTH REFERENCE

High temperatures across parts of Ireland

Hot conditions can increase dehydration and heat-stress risk, especially for vulnerable people.

  • Drink water regularly and choose hydrating foods.
  • Avoid prolonged outdoor activity during the hottest part of the day.
  • Use shade, light clothing and sun protection.
  • Seek urgent help for confusion, collapse or loss of consciousness.
